Nemotron-Labs-Diffusion

A single transformer that unifies autoregressive, diffusion, and self-speculation decoding within one set of weights. Modes are selected at inference time by swapping the attention pattern. Trained with a joint AR × diffusion objective at 3B, 8B, and 14B; base, instruct, and vision-language variants.

"AR and diffusion objectives are complementary: diffusion improves lookahead planning, while AR provides left-to-right linguistic priors. In self-speculation mode, diffusion drafts while AR verifies, outperforming multi-token prediction (MTP) methods in both acceptance rate and real-device efficiency." — NVIDIA Research publication abstract, May 19, 2026

Mechanism · 01

Mode switching by attention pattern, not by weights.

The base, instruct, and VLM checkpoints expose three generation entry points on a single set of parameters. Switching between AR decoding, diffusion-based parallel decoding, and linear self-speculation requires only changing the attention mask; the shared KV cache is reused across drafting and verification.

01 AR decoding

Causal next-token

Left-to-right sampling with a strictly causal mask. Used by model.ar_generate() on the model card. Latency-optimal at concurrency 1 when sequences are short; one token per forward pass.

02 Diffusion (dLM)

Block-bidirectional denoise

Masked-token denoising over blocks. The model fills a length-block_length window in parallel and iterates with a confidence threshold; multiple tokens land per forward pass.

03 Self-speculation

Diffusion drafts · AR verifies

The same network plays both roles: diffusion proposes a block of draft tokens in parallel, AR scores the block sequentially, accepted prefixes commit, the rest are rolled back. KV cache is shared across both paths.

Reading the attention masks

  • Bidirectional Token attends to all other tokens in its block — diffusion's lookahead.
  • Causal Token attends to itself and to all earlier positions — AR's prior.
  • Masked Attention forbidden (future or out-of-block positions).
  • Draft span Block of positions the model fills bidirectionally during the draft phase of self-speculation.

Training · 02

One model. Two losses. Trained jointly.

The publication frames the architecture as a joint AR-diffusion objective: the same parameters minimize next-token cross-entropy and a masked / denoising diffusion loss. NVIDIA reports the two objectives as complementary — diffusion contributing lookahead planning, AR contributing left-to-right linguistic priors.

Joint objective · schematic
L(θ) = LAR(θ)  + λ ·  LDIF(θ)
Schematic notation — the publication describes the objective as joint AR plus diffusion; the exact weighting λ and training schedule (curriculum, mixing ratio, masking schedule) are not reproduced verbatim here. See the technical report for specifics.
L_AR(θ)
Causal next-token cross-entropy. The standard autoregressive language modeling loss; provides left-to-right linguistic priors.
L_DIF(θ)
Diffusion / masked-token denoising loss over blocks. Enables bidirectional context and parallel decoding; provides lookahead planning.
θ
Shared transformer parameters. The same weights serve both objectives — no separate drafter network is trained.
What the joint training buys you

Mode synergy at inference

  • → AR contributes left-to-right linguistic priors — fluent token-by-token continuation.
  • → Diffusion contributes lookahead planning — global structure of a span before its tokens commit.
  • → Self-speculation rides on both: diffusion drafts, AR verifies, KV cache shared.
Mechanism · 03

Self-speculation: diffusion drafts, AR verifies.

Linear self-speculation runs the same network twice per step: a diffusion forward produces a length-block_length draft in parallel, then an AR forward scores the draft. The longest accepted prefix commits; the remaining positions are re-drafted on the next step. NVIDIA reports this beats Eagle3-style MTP on both acceptance length and real-device speed.

Model family · 04

Seven checkpoints. One architecture.

NVIDIA ships base, instruct, and a vision-language variant at the 3B/8B/14B scale points. All run the tri-mode interface. Tokenizer, context length, layer dimensions, and the VLM's vision encoder details are not published on the public Hugging Face pages at the time of writing — see Open questions.
